Overview

reveal-ck creates reveal.js presentation with a small, series of steps. Everything starts with your slides file.

If you author slide content in Markdown, your slides file will be called slides.md. If you write in html or haml your slides file will be called slides.html or slides.haml.

The Steps

The steps are:
  1. The reveal-ck gem contains a recent copy of reveal.js within it. This copy is unpacked into the slides/ directory.
  2. If you have files in directories named images/, css/, js/, or media/, these files are recursively copied into the slides/ directory.
  3. Your slides file is transformed into html and stored at slides/slides.html.
  4. Pieces of a reveal.js index.html are also stored within the reveal-ck gem. These pieces form together along with the content in slides/slides.html. The result is a stored at slides/index.html.
  5. The body of slides/index.html is run through a series of filters. These filters replace emoji.
